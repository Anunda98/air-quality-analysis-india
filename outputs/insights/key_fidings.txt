AIR QUALITY ANALYSIS (INDIA 2015–2020)

1. Overall Pollution Pattern
- Most cities fall in Moderate to Poor AQI range.
- Extreme pollution spikes exist but are less frequent.

2. Most Polluted Cities
- Certain cities consistently show higher AQI levels.
- Indicates strong influence of urbanization and industrial activity.

3. Key Pollutants
- PM2.5 and PM10 show strong correlation with AQI.
- These are the main drivers of air pollution levels.

4. Temporal Trends
- AQI varies across years with slight improvements in some periods.
- No consistent long-term improvement pattern observed.

5. Data Quality Notes
- Significant missing values in PM10 and AQI columns.
- Suggests inconsistent monitoring across stations.
